Originating in the 1930s, the Kecak dance is a form of Balinese Hindu dance and music drama. What makes it unique? No gamelan. No drums. Just the concentric circle of 150 men chanting "Cak" in a trance-like polyrhythmic choir.

ENTERPRISE CULTURAL PRESERVATION

The Choir

A human orchestra simulating the sounds of nature, war, and spirit.

02

The Story

Prince Rama's quest to save Sita from the clutches of Rawana.

03

The Fire

The trance finale where dancers walk through burning coconut husks.

04

The Temple

Performed daily at sunset on the cliffs of Uluwatu facing the ocean.
